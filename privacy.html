Privacy Policy for Combo Forge

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Introduction

Combo Forge ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you install and use our Shopify app.

By using Combo Forge, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Merchant Information

When you install the app, we may collect:

  • Store name
  • Store URL
  • Shopify store email address
  • Store owner details (if shared via Shopify API)

b. Usage Data

We collect data related to how you use the app:

  • Combo pages created
  • Templates used
  • Discounts configured
  • App interactions and performance data

c. Analytics Data

  • Page views
  • Conversion data
  • Revenue generated through combos
  • Feature usage behavior

d. Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Log data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Generate AI-powered content suggestions
  • Enable combo page creation and management
  • Deliver analytics and performance insights
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

4. AI Content Usage

Combo Forge includes AI-powered content generation features:

  • AI suggestions are generated based on your store data and selected templates
  • We do not use your private store data to train external AI models
  • Content generation is processed securely

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We may share data with:

  • Shopify (as required for app functionality)
  • Trusted third-party services (hosting, analytics, AI processing)
  • Legal authorities (if required by law)

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices
  • We implement encryption and access controls
  • We retain data only as long as necessary

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion

To request data deletion, contact us at: support@comboforge.com

Combo Forge may use third-party tools (e.g., analytics, AI services). These providers follow their own privacy policies.
